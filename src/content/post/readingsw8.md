[[PDF of Chapter 4](/docs/ncsai-ch4.pdf)] (The full report, which is over 700 pages is available here: [https://reports.nscai.gov/final-report/](https://reports.nscai.gov/final-report/).)

The memo on [Defense Transition](https://www.scsp.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Defense-Memo.pdf) is the most relevant.
